## 3. Guild Artifacts (Desk Items)
These are permanent items you keep on your desk to alter the game loop.

| Name | Effect | Risk/Cost | Flavour |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The All-Telling Eye** | **Reveal Traits**: Shows 1 Hidden trait (Drunk, Coward, etc) per adventurer. | High chance to offend privacy-minded heroes (-Rep). | "It blinks when you aren't looking." |
| **The Midas Quill** | **Double Gold**: All quest rewards x2. | **Cursed Contracts**: +10% Death Rate for adventurers signing with it. | "Ink turns to gold, blood turns to ink." |
| **Hourglass of Patience** | **Time Freeze**: Pauses day timer for 10s once per day. | Sand sometimes flows backward, undoing recent actions. | "Time is money. Steal both." |
| **Skull of the Founder** | **Advisor**: Whispers hints about quests. | **Madness**: Sometimes lies. | "He built this guild. He won't let it fall." |
| **Merchant's Scales** | **Haggle**: Can negotiate quest rewards up/down. | **Greed**: High chance characters reject the offer and leave. | "Perfectly balanced, as all things should be." |
| **Seal of the King** | **Authority**: Can command Royals/Guards. | **Tax**: Royal Tax Collector visits twice as often. | "Respect the crown, pay the toll." |
