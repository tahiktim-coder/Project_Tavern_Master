# Adventurer Registry

## 1. Martial Classes (The Frontline)
| Class | Specialty | Visual Traits | Hidden Traits (Common) | Strengths / Weaknesses |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Knight-Errant** | **Tank / Leader** | Shiny plate armor, heraldry on shield, well-groomed. | `Honorbound` (Refuses shady quests), `Arrogant`. | **+Survival** vs Monsters. **-Success** in Stealth. |
| **Barbarian** | **Damage** | Fur/Leather, scars, massive weapons, war paint. | `Battle-Rage` (Might kill hostage), `Illiterate`. | **+Combat** vs Beasts. **-Diplomacy** (Scares townsfolk). |
| **Mercenary** | **Versatile** | Mismatched armor, pragmatic weapons, coin purse visible. | `Greedy` (Demands extra gold), `Pragmatic` (Will retreat). | **+Reliable** if paid well. **-Loyalty** (Will defect if low HP). |
| **Pit Fighter** | **Duelist** | No armor, brass knuckles or unusual weapons, cauliflower ear. | `Sadist`, `Showman` (Takes risks for glory). | **+1v1 Combat**. **-Group Tactics**. |
| **Town Guard** | **Defender** | Generic uniform, polearm, looks tired. | `Lazy`, `Corrupt` (Accepts bribes). | **+Lawful** quests. **-Adventure** (Low stamina). |
| **Ronin** | **Burst Dmg** | exotic robes, curved blade, straw hat. | `Wanderer` (Leaves town after 1 day), `Disciplined`. | **+Critical Hit**. **-Communication** (Language barrier). |

## 2. Arcane Classes (The Glass Cannons)
| Class | Specialty | Visual Traits | Hidden Traits (Common) | Strengths / Weaknesses |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Battle Mage** | **AOE Damage** | Burned robes, smell of ozone, staff glows threateningly. | `Pyromaniac` (Burns loot), `Short-Tempered`. | **+Crowd Control**. **-Collateral Damage** (Quest giver house burns down). |
| **Scholar** | **Intel** | Glasses, many books, ink-stained fingers, no weapon. | `Cowardly`, `Curious` (Opens cursed chests). | **+Puzzle Solving**. **-Combat** (Dies instantly if hit). |
| **Necromancer** | **Summoner** | Pale skin, bone jewelry, smell of earth. | `Outcast` (Hated by priests), `Creepy`. | **+Attrition** (Skeletons fight for them). **-Reputation** (Town hates them). |
| **Illusionist** | **Trickster** | Colorful clothes, deck of cards, constant grin. | `Liar` (Fakes success), `Kleptomaniac`. | **+Stealth/social**. **-Undead** (Illusions don't work on them). |
| **Elementalist** | **Environmental** | Floating stones or dripping water around them. | `Volatile`, `Connected` (Talks to terrain). | **+Specific Biome** (Fire mage in volcano). **-Opposite Biome**. |
| **Hedge Wizard** | **Utility** | Herbs in pockets, simple wooden staff, looks like a gardener. | `Helpful`, `Unlicensed`. | **+Cheap**. **-Power** (Weak magic only). |

## 3. Cunning Classes (The Specialists)
| Class | Specialty | Visual Traits | Hidden Traits (Common) | Strengths / Weaknesses |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Rogue** | **Stealth** | Hooded, dark leather, daggers, silent footsteps. | `Greedy`, `Traitor` (Might steal the quest item). | **+Infiltration**. **-Fair Fight**. |
| **Ranger** | **Tracking** | Green cloak, bow, animal companion (Wolf/Hawk). | `Loner`, `Nature-Lover` (Won't kill rare beasts). | **+Travel/Open Field**. **-Dungeon/City**. |
| **Bard** | **Buff/Social** | Lute, bright clothes, handsome/beautiful. | `Drunk`, `Flirtatious` (Distracts party members). | **+Negotiation**. **-Combat** (Annoying). |
| **Assassin** | **Single Target** | Face mask, vials of poison, non-descript clothing. | `Cold-Blooded`, `Contract_Killer`. | **+Assassination**. **-Escort** (Safety not guaranteed). |
| **Treasure Hunter** | **Loot** | Backpack full of rope/torches, goggles, maps. | `Obsessive`, `Unlucky`. | **+Dungeons/Traps**. **-Combat** (Runs away with loot). |
| **Spy** | **Intel** | Changes appearance daily, average looking. | `Double Agent`, `Paranoid`. | **+Information Gathering**. **-Trust** (Who are they really?). |

## 4. Divine & Nature (The Support)
| Class | Specialty | Visual Traits | Hidden Traits (Common) | Strengths / Weaknesses |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Cleric** | **Healer** | Holy symbol, white/gold robes, mace. | `Zealot` (Might purge "sinners"), `Pacifist`. | **+Healing/Undead**. **-Stealth** (Clunky armor). |
| **Paladin** | **Tank/Smite** | Glowing armor, aura of light, loud voice. | `Lawful Stupid` (Must obey rules), `Judgemental`. | **+Demons/Undead**. **-Subtlety** (Glows in the dark). |
| **Druid** | **Shapeshifter** | Antlers, bark skin, no metal armor. | `Feral` (Prefers animal form), `Territorial`. | **+Wilderness**. **-Urban** (Hates cities). |
| **Monk** | **Unarmed** | Simple orange robes, prayer beads, calm demeanor. | `Vow of Silence`, `Disciplined`. | **+Demons** (Magic resist). **-Weapon Needs** (Can't use loot). |
| **Cultist** | **High Risk** | Robes with strange symbols, twitchy, whispering. | `Insane`, `Doomed`. | **+Forbidden Knowledge**. **-Sanity** (Might summon a horror). |
